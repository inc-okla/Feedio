Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Thank you for using Feedio APIs, a browser extension designed to help users automate API generation with AI platforms. We take privacy seriously — which is why our policy is intentionally straightforward: we do not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

TL;DR: The extension runs entirely on your device. No tracking, no analytics, no personal data collection. Ever.

What We Don’t Do

No Data Collection

Feedio APIs does not collect, log, or track any personally identifiable information. All actions performed in the extension stay on your device.

No Persistent Storage

The extension only stores small local preferences (model selection, last used fields) inside chrome.storage.local. No external databases are used.

No Monetization

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data. Our business model does not depend on surveillance.

Technical Details

Host Permissions

The extension injects a small script into these sites to read generated AI content:

  • https://firefly.adobe.com
  • https://labs.google

Request Processing

All API generation is processed locally or sent directly to the AI provider. No data is stored or cached.

Chrome Extension → AI Provider → Response → Deleted Immediately

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or advertising cookies.
